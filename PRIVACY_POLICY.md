# Privacy Policy for Habit Architect

**Last Updated:** January 3, 2026

## Introduction

Habit Architect ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Email address and display name when you create an account
- **Habit Data:** The habits you create, including names, categories, and scheduling preferences
- **Progress Data:** Daily success/failure logs and streak information
- **List Data:** Resistance lists (reasons not to do bad habits) and attraction lists (reasons to do good habits)

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data:** App interactions, feature usage patterns (anonymized)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the app's core functionality
- Track your habit progress and streaks
- Send notifications at your configured times
- Enable partner accountability features (if you choose to use them)
- Improve the app experience

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
All your personal data, including habits, logs, and resistance/attraction lists, is stored **locally on your device** using encrypted storage. We do not have access to this data.

### Cloud Authentication
We use Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in. This stores:
- Your email address
- Authentication tokens
- Basic profile information

## Data Sharing

### We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your habit data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Access your resistance/attraction lists (they remain private on your device)

### Partner Sharing
If you choose to use the Partner Accountability feature:
- Only habit names and streak counts are shared with your partner
- **Resistance and attraction lists are NEVER shared**
- You control which habits are shared
- You can revoke partner access at any time

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:
- Local data encryption on device
- Secure authentication through Firebase
- No transmission of sensitive content to external servers

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View all your data within the app
- **Export:** Export your data in JSON format via Settings
- **Delete:** Delete your account and all associated data
- **Control:** Choose what to share with partners

## Children's Privacy

Habit Architect is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
